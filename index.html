Privacy Policy

Last updated July 9, 2026

Lofye (Lofye: Lofi Focus Timer) is a focus timer app with music. This policy explains what data it collects and why. It's short because that's genuinely all there is.

What stays on your device

Your settings, session history, background/music selections, and custom reminders are stored locally on your device only. None of this is sent to us or anyone else, and we have no accounts or login system.

Who is responsible (data controller)

The data controller for this app is BergBuilds, reachable at bergbuilds@outlook.dk.

Crash & error reporting

If the app crashes or hits an error, technical details (device model, OS version, app version, and the error itself) are sent to Sentry, a crash-reporting service, so we can fix bugs. This data is hosted in the EU and does not include your IP address or a recording of your screen. We've explicitly turned both of those off.

Legal basis: we process this data under legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)), meaning our interest in keeping the app working and fixing bugs. Retention: crash data is kept by Sentry for up to 90 days, then deleted automatically.

Notifications

Daily reminders and Pomodoro alerts are scheduled entirely on your device. Nothing about your notifications is sent to a server.

Photos

If you share a stats card, the app can save that one generated image to your photo library. It cannot read or access your existing photos.

Feedback

Tapping "Send feedback" in the app opens your own email app with a message pre-filled to us. Anything you send goes through your email provider, same as any other email you send.

What we don't do

No ads, no third-party analytics or ad-tracking SDKs, no selling data to anyone. No automated decision-making or profiling of any kind.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to data portability. Crash data is the only data that leaves your device, and it isn't tied to your name or account (we have none). The practical way to exercise these rights is to email us, and we'll do our best to locate and act on any data associated with your device.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Denmark, that is Datatilsynet.

Questions

Contact us at bergbuilds@outlook.dk for privacy questions or to request deletion of any crash data tied to your device.
