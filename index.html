Privacy Policy

Last updated July 9, 2026

ProjectAlpha is a focus timer app with music. This policy explains what data it collects and why — kept short, because that's genuinely all there is.

What stays on your device

Your settings, session history, background/music selections, and custom reminders are stored locally on your device only. None of this is sent to us or anyone else, and we have no accounts or login system.

Crash & error reporting

If the app crashes or hits an error, technical details (device model, OS version, app version, and the error itself) are sent to Sentry, a crash-reporting service, so we can fix bugs. This data is hosted in the EU and does not include your IP address or a recording of your screen — we've explicitly turned both of those off.

Notifications

Daily reminders and Pomodoro alerts are scheduled entirely on your device. Nothing about your notifications is sent to a server.

Photos

If you share a stats card, the app can save that one generated image to your photo library. It cannot read or access your existing photos.

Feedback

Tapping "Send feedback" in the app opens your own email app with a message pre-filled to us. Anything you send goes through your email provider, same as any other email you send.

What we don't do

No ads, no third-party analytics or ad-tracking SDKs, no selling data to anyone.

Questions

Contact us at bergbuilds@outlook.dk for privacy questions or to request deletion of any crash data tied to your device.
