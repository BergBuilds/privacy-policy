Privacy Policy

Last updated July 13, 2026

Lofye (Lofye: Lofi Focus Timer) is a focus timer app with music. The short version: nothing you do in the app is collected or sent to us. This policy explains the details, and it's short because that's genuinely all there is.

What stays on your device

Your settings, session history, background/music selections, and custom reminders are stored locally on your device only. None of this is sent to us or anyone else, and we have no accounts or login system.

Who is responsible (data controller)

The data controller for this app is BergBuilds, reachable at bergbuilds@outlook.dk.

Notifications

Daily reminders and Pomodoro alerts are scheduled entirely on your device. Nothing about your notifications is sent to a server.

Photos

If you share a stats card, the app can save that one generated image to your photo library. It cannot read or access your existing photos.

Feedback

Tapping "Send feedback" in the app opens your own email app with a message pre-filled to us. Anything you send goes through your email provider, same as any other email you send.

What we don't do

No ads, no third-party analytics or ad-tracking SDKs, no selling data to anyone. No automated decision-making or profiling of any kind.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to data portability. In practice, Lofye collects no personal data and we hold nothing about you on any server (we have none and no accounts), so there is nothing for us to retrieve, share, or delete. If you have any questions about your rights, email us.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Denmark, that is Datatilsynet.

Questions

Contact us at bergbuilds@outlook.dk for any privacy questions.
